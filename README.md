## Part 1: Prompt Engineering for Mass Personalization

### Persona: COO of a Pharma SME
**AIDCA Structured Prompt:**
"Act as a B2B Growth Strategist. Write a personalized cold email to a COO of a mid-sized Pharma company using the **AIDCA** framework. 
- **Attention:** Start with a specific observation about the rising cost of regulatory compliance in 2026. 
- **Interest/Desire:** Bridge the gap between their manual audit stress and an automated system that recovers 15% of staff time.
